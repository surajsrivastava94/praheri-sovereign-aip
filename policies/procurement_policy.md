# Procurement Policy & Thresholds (sample — synthetic, vertical #2)

> Illustrative only. Demonstrates the Sovereign AIP engine reused for a second
> workflow: same ontology + action + governance pattern, new domain.

## Approval thresholds
- **Purchase orders within remaining department budget** may be auto-approved by an
  analyst, but are still audited.
- **Purchase orders that EXCEED remaining budget** require **finance-controller
  approval** before the contract is issued — the same human-in-the-loop gate the
  AML vertical uses for account freezes and STR filing.

## Vendor risk factors
- Vendor in a **high-risk jurisdiction** or flagged in sanctions screening.
- New vendor with no prior contract history.
- Requisition amount inconsistent with the stated description or department norms.

## Recommended dispositions
- **APPROVE** — within budget, known vendor, documented need.
- **ESCALATE** — over budget or high-risk vendor; route to the controller.
- **REJECT** — no budget, sanctioned vendor, or no legitimate business purpose.
